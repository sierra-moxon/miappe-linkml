    MIAPPE LinkML

    A LinkML representation of the MIAPPE (Minimum Information About a Plant Phenotyping Experiment) v1.1 checklist and data model. The structure follows the MIAPPE entity relationship diagram (Investigation > Study > Observation Unit > Sample) together with the supporting entities (Person, Publication, Biological Material, Environment Parameter, Experimental Factor, Event, Data File and Observed Variable). Each slot records its MIAPPE "codename" in a comment, and enumerations are derived from the MIAPPE checklist and its appendices.

    URI: https://w3id.org/sierra-moxon/miappe-linkml

    Name: miappe-linkml
